> Independent stewardship for sustainable software licensing.

The **Fair Community Initiative (FCI)** is the institutional body responsible for maintaining and evolving the Fair Community License (FCL) ecosystem.

FCI exists to provide structure, predictability, and transparency in the development of licensing models that balance openness, developer freedom, and long-term sustainability.

---

## üéØ Mission

To design, steward, and evolve licensing frameworks that:

- Protect creators from structural commercial imbalance
- Preserve developer freedom for learning and internal innovation
- Provide predictable and transparent governance
- Enable sustainable software ecosystems

## üß± Scope

The Fair Community Initiative is responsible for:

- Maintaining official FCL license texts
- Managing version updates and change processes
- Publishing governance policies
- Coordinating community consultation
- Documenting philosophical and structural principles
- Ensuring non-retroactive community trust commitments
